Proposals  for Cartenatex                                     
Overview 
This proposal outlines two website development options tailored for Cartenatex 
automotive service business. The goal is to establish a strong digital presence that 
showcases your services, builds credibility with clients, and supports future 
marketing growth — especially for customers under Bank Fleet, RMI, and AA 
programs.

Perfect for: Businesses that want a clean, functional online presence with essential 
information and branding. 
Features Included 
● Professional 5-page responsive website (Home, About, Services, Gallery, 
Contact) 
● Mobile-friendly design with modern visuals 
● Service booking/contact form connected to your email 
● Google Maps integration (to show workshop location) 
● Photo gallery to showcase workshop, team, and vehicles 
● Social media links (Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, etc.) 
● Basic SEO setup for Google visibility 
● 1 round of revisions
